NOVA USD FIXED INCOME FUND

The Nova USD Fixed Income Fund (“the fund”) is an actively managed open-ended unit trust scheme whose main objective is to achieve capital appreciation in the medium to long term for Investors with USD.

Benefits

  • Competitive returns.
  • An experienced fund management team.
  • Professional fund management utilizing a robust risk management framework.
  • With a minimum investment of USD 1,000, it gives investors access to US Dollar-denominated securities and better returns than the average domiciliary deposit account.
  • It is a viable option for currency diversification and long-term income generation.
  • Nova USD FIF is suitable for financial planning or other long-term major life events.
  • The fund will also give investors liquidity, diversification, annual income and professional portfolio management

PRICING

The issue price is $100 per unit.

The minimum initial investment for the offer is 5 units of the fund, while additional/subsequent investments will be issued in multiples of 5 units and payable in full, upon subscription.

MINIMUM HOLDING PERIOD

The minimum holding period of an investment in the fund is 180 days (6 months) from the date of subscription. However, an early redemption fee of 1.5% on the redemptive value of the units to be redeemed before the expiration of the minimum investment period.
