NOVA HYBRID FUND

The Nova Hybrid Fund is a balanced mutual fund created to maximize long-term capital growth and maintain regular income distribution. These funds are invested in Equities, Fixed Income and Money Market Instruments.

FEATURES / FUND’S
ASSET LOCATION

The Fund shall invest a maximum of 60% in quoted equities.

A combined maximum allocation of 5% to cash and other quality money market instruments

A minimum of 40% in a mix of short and long-term debt instruments of the Nigerian government.

PRICING

The minimum initial amount which you can invest in the Fund is
N10, 000.00

The minimum subsequent amount that you can invest is N5, 000.00.

Additional subscriptions can be made online via bank transfer into the fund’s account.

MINIMUM HOLDING PERIOD

A fee of 1.5% on the net proceeds is payable on any redemption effected within three (3) months of subscription to the Fund. After these first 3 months, there is no redemption charge.
